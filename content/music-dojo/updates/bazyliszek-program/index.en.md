Great news! Our application for the Warsaw fantasy festival **Bazyliszek 2026** has been officially accepted, and the schedule is set.

To ensure the best logistics and the highest quality of the session, we have decided to focus on a single, intensive workshop slot.

### When and Where?
*   **Date:** Saturday, July 11th, 2026
*   **Time:** 11:00 AM – 11:50 AM
*   **Location:** **Nikifor** Room, "Alternatywy" Ursynów Cultural Center (Warsaw)
*   **Workshop Title:** *MusicDojo – Make Your First Beat (and Musical Scrambled Eggs)*

### About the Workshop
This will be a fully hands-on, interactive session. We will show you that you don't need years of music school or expensive gear to make music – a smartphone in your pocket is more than enough! Together, we'll "fry up" our first musical scrambled eggs.

{{< alert icon="triangle-exclamation" >}}
**Important! Capacity Limit:** The Nikifor room has a maximum capacity of **30 people**. The workshop has limited seating, so make sure to arrive a few minutes early to secure your spot!
{{< /alert >}}

### Preparation – What to Bring?
1.  **Your own smartphone or tablet** (Android or iOS).
2.  The **Koala Sampler** app installed on your device (available on App Store and Google Play).
