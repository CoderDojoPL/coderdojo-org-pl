We are working in Koala Sampler! In the previous card, we created our own beat: a rhythm. Now we will save it as a recording.
{{< /lead >}}

If you do not have your own rhythm yet, go back to the previous card: [Create Your First Beat](../03-create-first-beat/).

## Step 1: Go to the Sequence Tab

Tap the **Sequence** button at the top of the screen. This is where we will create and record our rhythms.

![Sequence tab in Koala Sampler](sequence.jpeg)

## Step 2: Turn on Recording

Tap the **Record** button (the red dot under the word Sequence) and play your rhythm on the pads.

You can start with a simple rhythm made of just a few sounds.

To stop recording, tap **Record** again. To play back your recorded beat and hear the result, tap **Play** (the white triangle next to the dot).
