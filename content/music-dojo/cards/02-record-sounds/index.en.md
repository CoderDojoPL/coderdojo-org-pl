{{< lead >}}
We are working in Koala Sampler! In this task, we will record our own samples—the unique sounds we will use to create our very first piece of music.
{{< /lead >}}

You can learn more about the app and its features in the previous card: [Koala Sampler](../01-get-the-app/).

## Step 1: Start Recording

Tap and hold a pad of your choice (a red square) to start recording through your device's microphone.

![Starting recording](record.jpeg)

## Step 2: Record Your First Sample

Record a short sound from your surroundings. For example:
* a hand clap,
* a finger tap on the table,
* any funny sound you can make.

Your sound will be automatically saved under the selected pad.

## Step 3: Listen to Your Recording

Tap the exact same pad again (this time quickly) to hear your first sample!

---

## Mini Task

Using the next empty pads, record:
1. One **quiet** sound,
2. One **loud** sound,
3. One **very strange** sound.

Once you have completed this task, you will have samples recorded under each pad in the top row.

![Filled pads with samples](pads.jpeg)
