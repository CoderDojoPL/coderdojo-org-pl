{{< lead >}}
We are working in Koala Sampler! In this task, we will use the samples we recorded earlier to create our first beat: a simple rhythm that can become the foundation of a piece of music.
{{< /lead >}}

If you do not have your own samples yet, go back to the previous card: [Record sounds](../02-record-sounds/).

## Step 1: Play Your Samples

Tap the pads one by one and check how each sample sounds.

Listen for sounds that work well together.

![Pads with recorded samples](pads.jpeg)

## Step 2: Create a Rhythm

Try playing samples one after another.

For example:

* clap
* tap
* clap
* voice sound

Repeat this sequence a few times until you are happy with the result.

## Step 3: Find Your Rhythm

Change the order of your samples and try to create a rhythm you like best.

There is no single correct answer—experiment and have fun with the sounds!

---

## Mini Task

Create your own beat using at least 3 different samples.

When you find a rhythm you like, go to the next card: [Record Your Beat](../04-record-your-beat/).

---

{{< alert icon="lightbulb" >}}
**Tip:** Changing the order of your samples can completely change the sound of your beat.
{{< /alert >}}
